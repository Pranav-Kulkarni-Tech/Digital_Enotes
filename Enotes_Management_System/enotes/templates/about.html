About Us

This is Simple Enotes Management System Develop by Pranav Kulkarni. using Python Django, HTML, CSS, Bootstrp and sqlite.

This project is designed to help students create, organize, and store notes digitally. It eliminates the need for physical notebooks, ensuring that students never have to worry about losing their notes. With this system, students can access their notes anytime and anywhere
